**# Identity Design

## Project

Enterprise IAM Lab

## Company

BroncoTech Solutions

## Background

BroncoTech Solutions is a fictional technology company with approximately 200 employees.

The company recently migrated to Microsoft Entra ID to centrally manage identities, authentication, authorization, and access management.

As the Junior Identity & Access Management Analyst, I am responsible for designing and implementing the company's identity infrastructure.

---

# Objectives

- Design a scalable identity structure
- Implement Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
- Apply the Principle of Least Privilege
- Secure identities using Multi-Factor Authentication
- Standardize onboarding and offboarding
- Prepare the environment for PowerShell automation
